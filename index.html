Guess the Cards —Personal Project

I developed a simple card guessing game in C, titled 'Guess the Cards', as a demonstration of my programming skills. The program allows players to guess the sum of two randomly generated cards from two decks. Players and the computer take turns guessing the sum, with correct guesses resulting in winnings and incorrect guesses leading to deductions. The game is played over a user-defined number of rounds, with the winner determined by who accumulates the most winnings. This project showcases my proficiency in C programming and algorithmic thinking..
